About Project


On our website, users will be able to choose one of four different categories (photography, vector, illustration, video). They will also specify what they want to search for, and how many results they want to receive. We will utilize the Playwright library to automate web actions, allowing us to retrieve the desired number of keywords and titles from the Shutterstock website, and store this information in our database. We will then process the data from the database based on the user's selections. The resulting information will be presented on our site, including the most appropriate title and keywords. If the user wishes, they can also export this information as an Excel file.
